About StressSpec

Requirements Stress Tester

What is StressSpec?

StressSpec is a Python-based tool for analyzing requirement documents and detecting potential risks and issues before development begins. It acts as a "wind tunnel" for requirements, helping project managers, business analysts, and development teams identify hidden risks early in the development process.

Most software project failures stem from unclear, unrealistic, or incomplete requirements. Studies show that fixing requirement defects late can cost 5–10x more, and around 37% of enterprise project failures are linked to poor requirements.
